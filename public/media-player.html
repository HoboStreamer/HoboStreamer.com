How viewers use it

  • !sr https://youtube.com/watch?v=... adds media to the queue.
  • !yt, !youtube, !req, and !request work as aliases.
  • !queue shows the next few requests.
  • !nowplaying tells chat what is live.
  • Videos are streamed server-side — no embed restrictions or ads.
  • Coins are automatically refunded if playback fails.
